// LEARN THE BASICS //

Choose: a quick rundown, or jump into a guided practice flight.

1 · MOVE

A/D or ←/→ to strafe. W/S for forward thrust. Hold SHIFT to boost (consumes fuel).

2 · SHOOT

SPACE fires. Press C to cycle weapon mode: Plasma, Spread, Pulse, Railgun, Aether Wave.

3 · HEALTH

You start with 4 hearts. Asteroids and UFO bullets cost 1 heart each. Upgrade your hull in the shop for more.

4 · SHARDS

Smashing asteroids drops ◈ shards. Walk over them — your Grav Magnet upgrade pulls them in from range.

5 · POWERUPS

Glowing pickups: green + heal, yellow ◈ shield, pink ⚙ weapon swap, blue » rapid, red ! double damage, purple ⏱ slow time, orange ✕ bomb.

6 · BOSSES

Every 5th round is a boss. They enrage below 40% HP — get aggressive when their eye glows red.
